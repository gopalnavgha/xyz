**Tips for Customizing Your Resume:**

1. **Tailor to Job Descriptions:** Customize your resume for each job application by including keywords and skills mentioned in the job description.
   
2. **Highlight Achievements:** Focus on specific achievements and results from your internships, projects, and other experiences.

3. **Keep It Concise:** Aim for a one-page resume, especially as a recent graduate. Be concise but include all relevant information.

4. **Use Action Verbs:** Start bullet points with strong action verbs (e.g., "Led," "Developed," "Conducted") to convey your role and contributions effectively.

Feel free to adjust this template to better fit your personal experiences and the specific requirements of the positions you're applying for!
